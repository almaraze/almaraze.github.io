Survival Shooter Maze

You can play it here:

This game is a survival shooter like the title suggests. The player must survive as long as possible by running away from enemy zombies or killing them. The player can get health pickups or 2x damage powerups to try to survive longer. The zombies will flee from the player when the 2x damage powerup is enabled.
