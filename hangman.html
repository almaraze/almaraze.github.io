Hangman

Play Hangman:

I developed Hangman using Python to simulate the childhood classic classroom game by the same name. The game has nine different categories of words to guess from. The player starts with three lives allowing for longer gameplay. The game keeps the player's score and even keeps track of the all-time top scorers.
