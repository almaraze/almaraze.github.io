Cake Crusader

You can play the game here:

   

The battle system for Cake Crusader is a minigame of Rock, Paper, Scissors with standard rules. The player and the opponent both start with three lives. The player has two seconds to make a choice using the directional keys. The player can briefly see what the opponent is about to throw down and has to react quickly. Each opponent in the game has unique dialogue and the player is rewarded with new dialogue once the minigame is won.

   

One of the quest items that is required to bake a cake is milk. We decided to make it a little more challenging by adding a puzzle that simulates the shuffling of milk jugs around in order to obtain a non-expired jug. The rules of the game are that the blank space must end up in the bottom right corner and all pieces must end up in their correct position to form a picture. There are four slider puzzles in total and each one gets progressively more difficult. Once you complete all four puzzles you obtain the fresh milk.
