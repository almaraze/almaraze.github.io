Cake Crusader

You can play the game here:

   

The battle system for Cake Crusader is a minigame of Rock, Paper, Scissors with standard rules. The player and the opponent both start with three lives. The player has two seconds to make a choice using the directional keys. The player can briefly see what the opponent is about to throw down and has to react quickly. Each opponent in the game has unique dialogue and the player is rewarded with new dialogue once the minigame is won.
