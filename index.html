Cake Crusader

This is an adventure game about meeting unique characters, completing interesting challenges, and ultimitely
baking the perfect cake. This game was developed using GB Studio by me and a group of fellow Game Design students.
I was the lead programmer for this project. My major contributions for this project were the scripting for the Rock, Paper,
Scissors battle system and the scripting for the Milk Slider Puzzle. I also carefully curated the background music for all the scenes
and I created the sound effects using GB studio's internal sound effect component and custom scripts.
